Common questions

Before we get started.

Do I pay everything upfront?

Most projects use a staged payment schedule, typically beginning with a non-refundable deposit to reserve the project.

Are domain, hosting, printing, and filing fees included?

Third-party costs are normally paid directly by the client and are clearly identified before purchase.

Can I start smaller and add services later?

Yes. The best foundation is planned with growth in mind, even when the first engagement is focused.

Do you provide legal or tax advice?

No. Business launch assistance is administrative and technical only. Legal, tax, accounting, and entity-structure decisions remain with the client and their qualified advisors.
