Beauty & Wellness · Full launch

Beauty Lodge
by Anna.

A calm, elevated brand for a private beauty studio offering lash artistry, beauty enhancements, permanent makeup, and jewelry.

Scope

Brand refinement, web design, collateral, launch support

Platform

Custom HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Outcome

A cohesive client experience from discovery to booking

The challenge

Turn a service business into a memorable destination.

Beauty Lodge needed more than an attractive logo. The brand had to communicate warmth, quality, privacy, and trust while organizing a growing mix of services into a simple customer journey.

The project evolved into a complete business-facing ecosystem: visual identity, website architecture, booking integrations, team storytelling, service imagery, event promotion, print pieces, and launch messaging.

The approach

One visual language, everywhere.

A refined palette, editorial typography, consistent spacing, tailored imagery, and carefully simplified copy created a premium identity that remains approachable.
