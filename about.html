About Jake

Walnex Creative is built from both creativity and structure.

Founder Jake LaMotte began with a foundation in music theory and creative arts, where composition, rhythm, and presentation shape how ideas are experienced. His professional background later developed through physical security technology, digital forensics, investigations, and complex technical systems.

That combination gives Walnex its point of view: creative work should look good, but it should also make sense, function well, and support the business behind it.

Jake created Walnex to help startups, entrepreneurs, and small businesses build a polished presence from the beginning — with branding, websites, and digital launch support that make the business feel established, credible, and ready to grow.
