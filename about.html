About Jake

Technical thinking with a creative eye.

Walnex Creative is led by Jake LaMotte, a builder at heart with a background in technical systems, problem solving, and client-facing project work.

That mix shapes the way Walnex approaches every project: clean design, practical structure, and a focus on what actually helps a business grow. The work is not just about making something look good; it is about making the business easier to understand, easier to trust, and easier to move forward.

Jake strives to create brands and websites that feel intentional, premium, and useful. The goal is to give business owners a stronger first impression, a clearer message, and a digital presence they can be proud to send people to.

At its core, Walnex is about helping businesses move into their next stage with confidence.
