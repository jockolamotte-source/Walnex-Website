About Walnex

Creative thinking.
Technical precision.

Walnex is an independent brand and web studio based in West Michigan, built to give small businesses focused creative partnership without layers of agency overhead.

Why Walnex

Craftsmanship belongs in digital work.

The name combines the warmth and durability associated with walnut craftsmanship with a forward-looking idea of what comes next.

That balance shapes the work: timeless visual choices, modern technology, clear business thinking, and details that hold up beyond launch day.
